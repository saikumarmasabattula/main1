Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao (Sr. NTR)

Actor Profile: Sr. NTR

N.T. Rama Rao, popularly known as Sr. NTR, is the foundational pillar of Tollywood. Often referred to as "Viswa Vikhyatha Nata Sarvabhouma," he wasn't just a movie star; he was a cultural phenomenon who achieved a god-like status among the Telugu people.

He is most celebrated for his portrayal of mythological characters, particularly Lord Krishna and Lord Rama, which was so convincing that people would often worship his photos in their homes. Beyond his massive film career, he founded the Telugu Desam Party (TDP) and served as the Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, forever changing the political landscape of the region.
