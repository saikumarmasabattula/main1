Nandamuri Balakrishna - Nata Simham

Actor Profile: Nandamuri Balakrishna

Biography

Nandamuri Balakrishna (NBK), revered as "Nata Simham" (Lion of Acting), is the sixth son of legendary Sr. NTR. With over 100 films and 50+ years in cinema, he's renowned for thunderous dialogue delivery, high-energy action, and commanding screen presence.

Career Highlights

NBK's mass entertainers and mythological roles echo his father's legacy. His talk show Unstoppable with NBK became a cultural phenomenon, while recent blockbusters like Akhanda, Legend showcase his enduring mass appeal across generations.

Political & Social Service

As MLA from Hindupur (TDP) and Chairman of Basavatarakam Cancer Hospital, Balakrishna balances cinema stardom with dedicated public service, continuing the NTR family legacy.
