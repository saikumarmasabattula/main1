Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr. (Jr. NTR)

Actor Profile: JUNIOR NTR

Biography

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr., widely known as Jr. NTR, is a prominent figure in Indian cinema, celebrated for his exceptional acting range, powerful dialogue delivery, and mastery of the Kuchipudi dance form. As the grandson of the legendary actor-politician N. T. Rama Rao, he carries a massive legacy, yet he has established his own distinct identity as the "Man of Masses."

Career Journey

After starting as a child artist in the National Award-winning film Ramayanam (1997), his career as a lead actor took off with hits like Student No. 1 and Aadi, eventually leading to his status as one of the highest-paid actors in the country. His versatility is highlighted by his ability to handle complex roles, such as the triple characters in Jai Lava Kusa or the corrupt-cop-turned-vigilante in Temper.

Global Stardom

However, it was his portrayal of the revolutionary Komaram Bheem in S. S. Rajamouli's global blockbuster RRR (2022) that brought him unprecedented international recognition. His performance, particularly in the "Komaram Bheemudo" sequence, was lauded for its emotional depth and physical intensity. Beyond the big screen, Jr. NTR has also made a significant mark on television as the host of the first season of Bigg Boss Telugu and the game show Evaru Meelo Koteeswarulu.
