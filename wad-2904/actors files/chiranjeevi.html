Chiranjeevi - Megastar

Actor Profile: Chiranjeevi

Biography

Chiranjeevi, hailed as "Megastar," redefined Tollywood's commercial landscape with his unmatched dancing grace, high-octane action, and magnetic screen presence. He broke traditional acting molds to become the ultimate inspiration for generations of Telugu cinema stars.

Career Highlights

Rising in the late 1970s, Chiranjeevi became India's highest-paid actor, even surpassing Amitabh Bachchan at his peak. Over 150 films, he delivered massive commercial successes alongside critical acclaim, cementing his legendary status.

Legacy & Honors

Recipient of Padma Vibhushan (2024)—India's 2nd highest civilian award—Chiranjeevi's Chiranjeevi Charitable Trust has transformed countless lives. He served as Union Minister of Tourism (2012-2014) and remains Telugu cinema's eternal patriarch.
