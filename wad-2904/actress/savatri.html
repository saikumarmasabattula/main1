Actress Profile: Savitri (Mahanati)

Biography

Savitri, fondly known as "Mahanati" (The Great Actress), is widely regarded as the greatest actress in the history of Tollywood. She possessed a rare and extraordinary ability to express deep and complex emotions effortlessly, often conveying entire scenes through her eyes alone. Her performances continue to be studied and admired by actors even today.

First Female Superstar

She was the first female superstar of Telugu cinema, commanding remuneration equal to or even higher than her male counterparts such as N.T. Rama Rao (NTR) and Akkinenii Nageswara Rao (ANR). Her portrayal of Parvathi in Devadasu and the lively Sasirekha in Mayabazar are considered timeless masterpieces and benchmarks for acting excellence.

Multitalented Legend

Beyond acting, Savitri was also a director, producer, and playback singer. She was known for her extraordinary generosity, often helping colleagues and strangers without hesitation. Though her life was tragically short, her legacy remains immortal, and she continues to inspire generations of artists.
